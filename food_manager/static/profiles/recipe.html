Recipe Resource Profile

This resource represents a recipe associated with a specific food item, including instructions and preparation details.

Fields

FieldTypeRequiredDescription
recipe_idintegerNoUnique ID of the recipe
food_idintegerYesForeign key referencing the associated food
instructionstringYesCooking instructions
prep_timeintegerYesPreparation time (minutes)
cook_timeintegerYesCooking time (minutes)
servingsintegerYesNumber of servings
nutritional_infoobjectNoNutritional details
ingredientsarrayNoList of ingredients with quantities and units
categoriesarrayNoList of associated categories
